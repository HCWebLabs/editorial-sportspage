    Gameday Guide

    Getting There

    • Rideshare or public lots; expect road closures near campus.
    • Plan 60–90 minutes for traffic and entry queues.
    • Pick a meetup spot in case phones die.

    Food & Water

    • Many venues are cashless—bring a card or mobile pay.
    • Hydrate early; find refill spots on concourses.
    • Family sections are great with kids; consider ear protection.

    Entry & Bags

    • Many venues have clear bag rules and metal detectors.
    • Leave large items, outside alcohol, and prohibited items at home.
    • Always verify with the official event page before you go.

    Accessibility

    • Check ADA parking, shuttle drop-offs, elevators, and seating.
    • Service animals typically permitted—follow venue guidance.
    • Contact accessibility office ahead for assistance.

    Fan News

    • Vol Walk starts 2 hours before kickoff — meet near Gate 21.
    • Campus concessions testing a new refill station in Section 128.
    • Pep band pops-up on the south concourse at halftime (unofficial).

    Tailgate Tips

    • Arrive early; prime lots fill 3–4 hours pre-game.
    • Bring water + shade; campus heat reflects off concrete.
    • Pack out trash — orange bags available at lot attendants.

    Visiting Fans

    • Away sections typically in the upper northeast — check your row for nearest ramp.
    • City trolleys run pre/post game — look for green route signs.
    • Family-friendly eats: Market Square has quick options post-game.

    Transportation

    • Most campus lots are permit-only on gameday. Public cash lots open along Cumberland Ave.
    • Expect closures around Phillip Fulmer Way 2.5h pre-game.
    • Rideshare pickup zone moves to Clinch Ave after the 4th quarter.
