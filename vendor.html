How to Submit a Gameday Deal

This is an unofficial Tennessee football fan site. We list community-submitted gameday deals to help fans plan their day. Listings are free, curated, and not guaranteed.

Who should submit?

  • Restaurants, bars, cafés, bakeries
  • Retailers with gameday promos (merch, parking, tailgate supplies)
  • Venues with watch parties, live music, or pre/post-game events
  • Family-friendly options and brunch spots

What to include

  • Deal title (short + punchy): “Wings + Pitchers”, “Post-Game Pancakes”
  • Description (what’s included, exclusions, dine-in/takeout)
  • Valid days: “Gameday only”, “Fri–Sun”, or “All week”
  • Time window: e.g., 4pm–7pm or Kickoff → final whistle
  • Dates (optional): start/end date if it’s seasonal
  • Area: Downtown, Old City, The Strip, West Knoxville, etc.
  • Link to menu/details and a phone number
  • Address (optional but helps) — add lat/lng for map pins

Formatting tips

  • Keep titles ≤ 60 characters; descriptions ≤ 200 where possible
  • Use one clear offer per listing (you can submit more than one)
  • Time format examples: 10am–2pm, 9:30pm–1am
  • Family/age rules: note IDs for alcohol, minors after certain hours, etc.

Approval & updates

  • We review for clarity and may edit wording before publishing
  • Listings can be featured at our discretion
  • Need a change or removal? Email listings@yourdomain.com
